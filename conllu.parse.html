parse-avm

(parse-avm s c kf vf)

parses an attribute-value matrix s into a map. the attribute-value pairs are linked by c, and separated by |. the keys are transformed by kf and the values by vf.

(parse-avm "a=1|b=2|c=3" \= keyword parse-nat-int)
#_=> {:a 1, :b 2, :c 3}
